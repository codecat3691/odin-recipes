Green Goddess Salad

How to Make The Green Goddess Salad

A simple romaine salad topped with classic green goddess dressing made with mayonnaise, parsley, spinach, basil, tarragon, and lemon juice.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups chopped green cabbage
  • 4 cups chopped hearts of romaine
  • 3 medium Persian cucumbers, sliced
  • ½ cup thinly sliced green onions
  • 1 medium ripe avocado, peeled and chopped
  • ¼ cup chopped roasted, salted pistachios

Steps

  1. Combine spinach, parsley, basil, chives, tarragon, shallot, garlic, anchovy paste, and lime juice in a high-powered blender.
  2. Blend and slowly add oil through top opening of blender until mixture is smooth, about 1 minute, scraping down sides if needed.
  3. Add mayonnaise and salt; blend until smooth. Cover and chill until ready to serve.
  4. Combine cabbage, romaine, cucumber, and scallions in a large bowl; add 2/3 cup of the dressing and toss to evenly coat.
  5. Divide between 4 bowls and top each bowl with avocado and 1 tablespoon of the pistachios.
  6. Serve immediately with remaining dressing.
